Training Modules Overview

Your training consists of several key modules. Here's what to expect and how to prepare:

Basic Field Operations

Includes: Traffic Stops, Scene Control, Use of Force

Key Focus: Safety protocols, proper positioning, clear communication

Tactical Operations

Includes: CQB, Breaching, Pursuit Driving

Key Focus: Team coordination, tactical movement, situation awareness

Investigation Skills

Includes: Evidence Handling, Interrogation, Case Management

Key Focus: Detail orientation, documentation, legal procedures

Special Operations

Includes: Surveillance, Undercover Work, Hostage Negotiation

Key Focus: Patience, adaptability, stress management

Standard Operating Procedures

Traffic Stops

  • Call in plate before initiating stop
  • Position vehicle offset for safety
  • Maintain visual on occupants
  • Use spotlight during night stops
  • Approach from driver's side unless unsafe

    • Building Entry

  • Establish perimeter control
  • Designate roles (point, cover, rear)
  • Clear communications for movement
  • Maintain weapon discipline
  • Mark cleared rooms

    • Evidence Handling

  • Photograph before collection
  • Wear appropriate PPE
  • Document chain of custody
  • Proper packaging and labeling
  • Maintain evidence integrity

    • Training Success Tips

    Physical Preparation

  • Maintain good physical fitness
  • Practice weapon handling regularly
  • Get adequate rest before training
  • Stay hydrated during exercises

    • Mental Preparation

  • Study procedures before practical exercises
  • Ask questions when unclear
  • Learn from mistakes
  • Stay focused during scenarios
    • Take detailed notes during briefings and debriefings. Review them regularly.

    Expectations & Standards

    Area Expectation
    Professionalism Maintain professional demeanor at all times, respect chain of command
    Communication Clear, concise radio traffic; proper use of codes and callsigns
    Teamwork Support fellow officers, follow orders, contribute to team success
    Documentation Thorough, accurate reports; proper evidence handling
    Safety Follow all safety protocols; maintain weapon discipline
    Violations of core protocols or safety standards may result in immediate removal from the program.
